About us
We are an engineering company specializing in DevOps integration for companies of all sizes and fields. At OpsWorks Co. we strive to help our clients build scalable and easily manageable IT infrastructures. As a DevOps service provider, we have optimized infrastructures for 100+ clients from the U.S. and EU over the past two years.

Team History

Our CEO, Artem Dolobanko, made his career path from a System Administrator to DevOps Engineer. After his first big contract he founded OpsWorks Co.

The first to join were his university friends, another DevOps Engineer and a System Administrator. OpsWorks Co. has been a team ever since.

OpsWorks Co. team has reduced the maintenance cost of a huge cloud infrastructure by 40%. The quality work has attracted a lot of new clients since then.

Today, OpsWorks Co. is a team of 50+ people, a loft office in the Kyiv city centre, and a desire to bring digital transformation to the world.
